Delivery Information

At Pete's Pan Pizza, we offer fast and convenient delivery services to ensure you can enjoy our delicious pizzas in the comfort of your own home or office.

Our delivery area covers most neighborhoods within a 5-mile radius from our location. Simply enter your address during the checkout process to see if we deliver to your area.

Delivery Hours

Our delivery service is available during the following hours:

  • Monday to Thursday: 11:00 AM - 9:00 PM
  • Friday to Saturday: 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM
  • Sunday: 12:00 PM - 9:00 PM

Delivery Charges

We offer free delivery on orders above $20 within our delivery area. For orders below $20, there is a flat delivery fee of $5.

Delivery Time

Our delivery time varies based on the volume of orders and traffic conditions. We strive to deliver your order as quickly as possible, usually within 30-45 minutes.

Please note that delivery times may be longer during peak hours and inclement weather conditions.

Payment Options

We accept cash and major credit cards for payment upon delivery. For online orders, you can also use our secure payment gateway to pay with your credit or debit card.
